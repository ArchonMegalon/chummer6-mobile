## Intent

The new lane turns the current campaign OS direction into a future world-state engine where factions, districts, projects, pressure, and mission-market packets create tension around campaigns without replacing campaign truth, rule-environment receipts, or GM authority.

## Immediate recommendation

Do not implement human faction seats first.

Reserve the future contract family, reserve the role vocabulary, widen rule-environment semantics for explicit world-linked offers and modifiers, and start any eventual build path with a GM-only world engine.
